How It Works

From farm to your cup —
every step recorded.

Our blockchain-anchored QR system creates an immutable record for every batch. No greenwashing. No vague claims. Just verifiable facts.

01
🌱
Harvest Recorded

When cacao pods are harvested at our partner farms in Gia Lai and Lâm Đồng, a digital record is created: GPS coordinates, date, farmer ID, and lot number.

02
⚗️
Processing Logged

Fermentation duration, drying method, and quality test results are documented at the processing stage. Every temperature log and moisture reading is stored.

03
🏭
Production Tagged

Stone grinding, tempering, and packaging at our facility in Spain is linked to the original batch. A unique QR code is printed on every block.

04
📱
You Verify

Scan the QR on your block. In seconds, you see the complete chain — who grew it, where, when, and how. The full record is yours to explore.
