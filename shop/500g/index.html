Grown in the highlands of Buon Ma Thuot, Vietnam, at 800m above sea level under natural shade canopy — our Ceremonial Cacao is crafted to transform your daily ritual. Made from heirloom Forastero cacao beans native to the region.

Unlike industrial cacao, our beans are stone-ground at low temperature with zero additives. The result is a high-theobromine paste that provides gentle, sustained energy and mental clarity without the crash of caffeine.

Rich earthy notes layered with subtle fruit and floral undertones — this cacao is a perfect companion for morning ceremonies, meditation, yoga, or simply a mindful cup to start your day.

How to Prepare

4 steps to the perfect cup

1
Measure

Chop or grate 30g of cacao per cup. For a full ceremonial experience, use up to 42g.

2
Heat

Warm your milk or water to 70–75°C. Do not boil — high heat can degrade the theobromine structure.

3
Blend

Add cacao to the hot liquid and whisk or blend until frothy. Season with cinnamon, cayenne, or honey to taste.

4
Intend

Hold the cup in both hands, take a few deep breaths, and set an intention for your day before the first sip.
